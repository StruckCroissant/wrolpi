Setup instructions

You will first need to download your free map data. This can be a single state, an entire sub-region, or even the entire planet.

  1. Proceed to this external website and choose one of their free PBF files. Copy it's URL.
    • Beware! This map will be stored on your WROLPi's hard drive in a docker container; don't choose a file that is larger than your drive.
    • For example, I could paste 
      https://download.geofabrik.de/north-america/us-west-latest.osm.pbf
      into the form below, and I would have a map for the entire US West Coast.

  3. this form to download and import your chosen map data!
    • If you're re-submitting your chosen file, the download will continue from where it left off.

This will take quite some time (several hours if you've chosen a large file). Come back later and try out your shiny offline map!
